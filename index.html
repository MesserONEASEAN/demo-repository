About ONE ASEAN

ONE ASEAN represents the collective vision of unity, collaboration, and digital transformation across the Association of Southeast Asian Nations (ASEAN). Our mission is to foster regional cooperation through innovative technology solutions and shared digital infrastructure.

Key Objectives

  • Promote digital connectivity across ASEAN member states
  • Facilitate cross-border collaboration in technology and innovation
  • Support sustainable development through digital transformation
  • Strengthen regional economic integration

ASEAN Member Countries

Brunei Cambodia Indonesia Laos Malaysia Myanmar Philippines Singapore Thailand Vietnam

Building bridges, fostering innovation, creating a unified digital future for Southeast Asia.
